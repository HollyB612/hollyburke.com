Walk-Behind Floor Scrubbers

Walk-behind floor scrubbers are compact and easy to use battery powered units. They deliver cost-effective, day-to-day cleaning for both small and large jobs in hospitals, schools, hotels, manufacturing plants, warehouses and more.

Walk-behind floor scrubbers offer:

  • Easy-to-maneuver platform, interchangeable scrub decks and traction-drive operation.
  • Easy access to the battery, recovery tank, and detergent dispensing system for easy maintenance.
  • Over 1.5 hours of run time between dump and refill, allowing for increased productivity.

Ride-On Sweepers

Ride-on sweepers are also called rider sweepers and provide maximum performance and reliable cleaning solutions for outdoor areas such as parking lots, sports centers, and recycling facilities.

Ride-on sweepers offer:

  • Maximum performance and reliability designed to improve productivity, simplify operation, and minimize downtime.
  • A safe and efficient retractable hopper and hydraulic high dump that allows you to get more done in less time.
  • Intuitive front wheel steering and front wheel drive, enabling you to maneuver in tight corners and narrow aisles.
