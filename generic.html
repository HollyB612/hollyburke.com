These are some of the assets to the video game I created. I used vector images in Adobe Photoshop. I animated these characters and created the final product game with Gdevelop. Time constraints in class allowed me only to complete one level, but throughout this single level, the gamer is allowed to make the characters jump around obstacles, collect items for points, and defeat the villain. I hand-drew all of these sprites and then traced them in Adobe Photoshop.
