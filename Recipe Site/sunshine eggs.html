Sunshine Eggs



Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • A pad of butter

Instructions

  1. Cut a small circle in the middle of your slice of bread.
  2. In frying pan, place a pad of butter. Using low heat spread butter around pan
  3. Place bread slice in pan. Crack egg into the hole of the bread
  4. Cook on one side for 5 minutes.
  5. Flip bread & egg and cook on other side for 5 minutes
  6. Remove from pan nad enjoy!
