Pineapple Smoothie<



Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups of Cubed Pineapple, Frozen
  • 1 Banana, sliced and frozen
  • 1 Cup of Canned Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 TSP. of Coconut Extract
  • 1/2 TSP. of Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Combine pineapple, banana, coconut milk, coconut extract, and vanilla in a blender cup; blend until smooth.
  2. Serve immediately.
