Chickan Divan



Ingredients

  • 3 Whole Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Packages of Frozen Broccoli
  • 2 Cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 Cup of Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Celery
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 8 oz. of sour Cream
  • 1 Cup of Mayo
  • 1 TBSP. of Lemon Juice

Instructions

  1. Boil chicken with celery and onion until tender.
  2. Cool & cut into bite size pieces.
  3. Cook broccoli.
  4. Grease 9x13 pan.
  5. Spread drained broccoli on bottom, then add the chicken.
  6. Mix soup, sour cream, mayo, cheese, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste. Pour over chicken.
  7. Sprinkle parmesean cheese, dot with butter.
  8. Bake 30-40 minutes at 350 degrees uncovered.
