French Toast Bake



Ingredients

  • 1 Loaf of Bread
  • 8 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1/2 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 3/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 TBSP. of Vanilla

Topping

  • 1/2 Cup of Flour
  • 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 TSP. of Cinnamon
  • 1/4 TSP. of Salt
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter (cut into pieces)

Instructions

  1. Cut bread into cubes. Grease 9x13 pan.
  2. In abowl mix eggs, milk, cream, sugar, and vanilla.
  3. Pour over bread, tightly cover, refridgerate over night.

Topping

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Cut in butter.
  3. Seal in bag - refridgerate over night too.

Next Day

  1. Take out pan, sprinkle crumbly mixture on top.
  2. Bake at 350 degrees for 45min - 1 hour.
