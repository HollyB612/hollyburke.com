Gingerbread Hot Cocoa



Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Cocoa
  • 1 Cup of White Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 2 TSP. of Ground Ginger
  • 1 TSP. of Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 TSP. of Ground Cloves
  • 1/4 TSP. of Salt
  • 1 Pinch of Cayenne Pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add cocoa, white sugar, brown sugar, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, salt, and cayenne to a bowl, and whisk thoroughly to combine.
  2. Store in an airtight container until needed. Be sure to stir very thoroughly before each use.
  3. To make hot chocolate, heat milk over medium heat until it almost starts to simmer.
  4. Whisk in 2 tablespoons of instant gingerbread hot chocolate mix per 1 cup milk.
  5. Once everything is mixed together and dissolved, and the milk is piping hot, transfer into mugs and enjoy.
