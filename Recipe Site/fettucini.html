Fettucini



Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Noodles
  • 1/4 Cup of Salad Oil
  • 1/8 TSP of Garlic
  • 1 Cup of Cottage Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup of Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup of Condensed Chicken Broth (undiluted)
  • 1/2 TSP of Basil Leaves
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Parsley
  • 10 oz. Chopped Broccoli (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook and drain noodles.
  2. Saute garlic and broccoli in salad oil for 5 minutes.
  3. Add all ingredients except the noodles. Stir over low heat until blended, about 2 minutes.
  4. Toss mixture with noodles.
