Scones



Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 Cups of Flour
  • 1/2 Cups od Sugar
  • 3/4 TSP. of Salt
  • 1 TBSP. of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 Cups of Buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in butter, to make a crumble. Set aside.
  4. Beat egg slightly.
  5. Add in buttermilk.
  6. Add in flavor ingredient (chocolate chips, fruit, etc) to the dry mix.
  7. Add in the egg and buttermilk mixture.
  8. Bake 16-18 mins.
