Apple, Strawberry, and Banana Smoothie



Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup of Vanilla Almond Milk
  • 1 Apple - peeled, cored, and cubed
  • 1 Cup of Halved Frozen Strawberries
  • 1 Frozen Banana, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 Cup of Vanilla Greek Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Place almond milk, apple, strawberries, banana, and yogurt in a blender, being sure the milk goes in first.
  2. Process until smooth, occasionally scraping down the sides of the blender with a rubber spatula, if necessary.
  3. Pour into serving glasses and serve immediately.
