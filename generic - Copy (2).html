In this endeavor, with my team we created a smooth flowing application with Adobe XD for tourists and residents to find parks near them. I created different mock-ups for the design and then once our design was decided on, I made sure the colors, fonts, etc. matched throughout the different pages of the application. We researched for actual pictures of the specific parks for their pages. We added interactions using the prototyping in XD to provide transitions for users. We redesigned multiple icons, so that they were more mobile friendly since this is a mobile application.
